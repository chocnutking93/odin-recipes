Garlic Butter Chicken

Buttery lickin good

If you're in need of a fast and easy 15-minute chicken dinner, this comforting garlic butter chicken breast recipe will be a new family favorite. It's made in one skillet, which is great because there's less cleanup.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 21 Salute Seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • White win or chicken broth
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Fresh parsley, optional

Recipe Instructions

  1. To a large skillet, add olive oil, chicken breasts seasoned with 21 Salute Seasoning, salt, pepper, and sear on the first side for about 5 minutes. Flip chicken oven and cook on the second side for about 5 minutes.
  2. When the chicken is done and cooked through, remove it from the pan and allow it to rest on a plate.
  3. Deglaze your pan with a splash of wine, add the butter, garlic, and cook for one minute, or until the butter has melted and the garlic is fragrant.
  4. Return the chicken to the pan, toss it in the garlic butter sauce, garnish with parsley, and serve. I told you this was fast and easy!
